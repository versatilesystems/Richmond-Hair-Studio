OUR TEAM

Joanne

Joanne has operated the Richmond Hair Studio (formerly Richmond Beauty Salon) since 1990. Her passion for hairdressing started at a very young age. Joanne enjoys providing hair services to new clients as well as 2nd and 3rd generation clients. Joanne’s life has been fulfilled by many excellent teachers and co-stylists. The Westboro area has evolved like The Richmond Beauty Salon and she says: "I have been well blessed to be a part of both. Thank you everyone!"

Rezzi

Rezi has worked as a licensed hair stylist for over 14 years, the last 7 of which have been with Richmond Hair Studio. She enjoys helping customers achieve the look they desire.

Shelley

Shelley joined the staff at Richmond Hair Studio in 1990. She provides her clients with expertise gained from 20+ years as a licensed hair stylist. Shelley has received aditional training in Advanced coloring, cutting and foiling with MATRIX and JOICO. Shelley’s outgoing nature is infectious to all.

Umna

"The same color or perm treatment will behave differntly on different hair. For this reason we use differnt products with different application techniques in different situations" says Umna. She is a licensed hair stylist and specialist in coloring and texturizing hair and has a unique combination of training and experience in both the chemistry and application of different hair treatments. She is able to consitantly achieve optimum results for our clients. Umna joined Richmond Hair Salon in 2005 after a prior career as a social worker in Pakistan.
